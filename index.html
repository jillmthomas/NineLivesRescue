Donut Delights founder, Melvin Furballski, was known far and wide in his homeland of Poland for his delicate pastries

In 1970, he fled to America in search of a better life for his family. Melvin landed in New York City and caught a bus heading west. An unexpected mechanical failure forced a three day delay in Urbana, Illinois where Melvin saw the need for a donut shop. >
